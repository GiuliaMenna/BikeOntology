BikeOntology

The Idea

BikeOntology is a project building an ontology about bicycles, born by the work of three bike lovers living in the city of Bologna.
The idea was to create a useful tool describing the characteristics of different types of bikes, deepening which problems can affect a bike and how they can be solved changing or adjusting some pieces. Surfing on the web was possible to find some interesting ontologies on vehicles in general, but nothing specific about bicycles. From the absence of an existing ontology on this matter and thinking on the usefulness of creating a new one the project began.

Per un buon pezzo, la costa sale con un pendii lento e continuo; poi si rompe in poggi e in valloncelli, in erte e in ispianate,secondo l’ossatura de’ due monti, e il lavoro dell’acque. Il lembo estremo, tagliato dalle foci de’ torrenti, è quasi tutto ghiaia e ciottoloni; il resto, campi e vigne, sparse di terre, di ville, di casali; in qualche parte boschi, che si prolungano su per la montagna.
