Introduction back to ToC

The initial purpose of BikeOntology was to create a useful tool describing the characteristics of bikes in general, deepening the mechanical structure mutual to every model. Then, the project evolved through the implementation of some problems that could affect a bike and how they can be fixed by changing or adjusting some pieces. Surfing the web there were some interesting ontologies on vehicles in general, but nothing bike specific. The Automotive ontology, Schema, and The Vehicles Sales Ontology were very useful sources for taking inspiration and reusing some existing data.
